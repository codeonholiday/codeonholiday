✏️ Draw — Annotate in the moment

Press a hotkey and your cursor becomes a pen. Draw freely, add arrows, create rectangles. Choose colors, adjust stroke width. Set drawings to auto-fade or keep them until you dismiss. Perfect for highlighting key points during live presentations without breaking flow.

✏️

❄️ Freeze & Explain — Pause time

Capture the current screen as a frozen image, then draw on top. Your audience sees the snapshot while you annotate and explain at your own pace. No more rushing while content changes underneath you.

❄️

🎨 Whiteboard — Your private canvas

Open an infinite whiteboard without leaving your presentation. Add shapes, lines, text, freehand drawings. Select, move, resize, rotate. Full undo/redo history. Export to PNG, SVG, or Excalidraw format. All data stays local — no account required.

🎨

⏱️ Break Timer — Keep everyone on schedule

Set a countdown timer overlay for breaks. Choose fullscreen or minimal corner display. Customize the message. Optional chime when time's up. Perfect for workshops, training sessions, and long presentations.

⏱️

🎯 Cursor Highlight — Be seen

Add an animated halo around your cursor. Customize color, size, and animation style. Click effects show where you're interacting. Essential for screen recordings, demos, and helping others follow along.
