Five tools, zero friction

Click through each workflow — every one is a single hotkey away, layered right over your screen.

Annotate in the moment

Press a hotkey and your cursor becomes a pen. Draw freely, add arrows and rectangles, pick colors and stroke width. Set drawings to auto-fade or keep them until you dismiss.

  • Pen, arrows, rectangles, freehand
  • Auto-fade or persistent drawings
  • Works over slides, browsers, and video calls

Pause time

Capture the current screen as a frozen image, then draw on top. Your audience sees the snapshot while you annotate and explain at your own pace — no more rushing while content changes underneath you.

  • One-key freeze of the live screen
  • Numbered annotations for step-by-step walkthroughs
  • Resume the live screen when you're done

Your private canvas

Open an infinite whiteboard without leaving your presentation. Add shapes, lines, text, and freehand drawings. Select, move, resize, rotate — with full undo/redo. All data stays local, no account required.

  • Shapes, text, connectors, freehand
  • Export to PNG, SVG, or Excalidraw format
  • Local-only — no cloud, no signup

Keep everyone on schedule

Set a countdown timer overlay for breaks. Choose fullscreen or a minimal corner display, customize the message, and add an optional chime when time's up. Perfect for workshops and long sessions.

  • Fullscreen or minimal corner overlay
  • Custom messages and end chime
  • Pause or add minutes on the fly

Be seen

Add an animated halo around your cursor. Customize color, size, and animation style, with click effects that show exactly where you're interacting. Essential for screen recordings, demos, and remote teaching.

Questions

Before you ask

Does it work while I'm sharing my screen on Zoom, Meet, or Teams?
Yes. HoverBoard draws and overlays on top of whatever is on your screen, so when you share your screen or a window in any video call, your audience sees the annotations live. It works over Keynote, PowerPoint, browsers, IDEs — anything.
Does my data stay private?
Completely. HoverBoard has no backend server, no account, and no telemetry on your screen contents. Your slides, drawings, and whiteboards stay on your Mac. Whiteboards export only when you choose to.
What's the refund policy?
A 30-day, no-questions-asked money-back guarantee. If HoverBoard doesn't fit your workflow within 30 days of purchase, email us and we'll refund the full amount — you don't need to justify it. Buy it, put it through its paces in real presentations, and if it's not for you, get every cent back.
Which macOS version do I need?
HoverBoard runs on macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later. It's a lightweight native menu bar app that uses minimal resources and stays out of the way until you summon it.
Can I customize the hotkeys?
Yes. Every tool — Draw, Freeze, Whiteboard, Break Timer, Cursor — has a global hotkey, and all of them are remappable in Settings to whatever fits your muscle memory. Defaults are ⌘⇧D / F / W / B / C.
Can I export my whiteboards?
Yes. Whiteboards export to PNG, SVG, or Excalidraw format, so you can drop them straight into your slides, docs, or hand off the source file for further editing.
