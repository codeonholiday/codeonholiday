Questions

Before you ask

Does Meetly work with my calendar?
If it shows up in macOS Calendar — Google, Outlook, iCloud, Exchange, and more — Meetly can watch it. The Free plan monitors one calendar, perfect for a single work or personal calendar. Pro unlocks unlimited calendars so you can track work and personal side by side. Meetly reads events to remind you; it never modifies your calendars.
Is my calendar data safe?
Yes. Meetly has no backend server. Your calendar data stays on your Mac, and sync state (dismiss/join/snooze) travels only through your own iCloud when you enable Pro. Nothing is sold, shared, or sent to us.
Do I have to pay every month?
Never. The Free plan is free forever and covers single-Mac use completely. Pro is a one-time $3.99 payment (launch pricing, normally $9.99) that unlocks iCloud sync across your Macs. No subscription, no recurring charge.
What if I work on multiple Macs?
That's exactly what Pro is for. With iCloud sync, dismissing or joining a meeting on one Mac marks it handled on all your Macs — no more double reminders for the same meeting. Pro also keeps your reminders private while you're screen sharing. Single-Mac use stays fully free.
Is Meetly a replacement for my calendar app?
No, and that's intentional. Meetly does one thing exceptionally well: making sure you never miss an important meeting. It works alongside your calendar app, not instead of it. Keep using Google Calendar, Fantastical, or whatever you love.
What macOS version do I need?
Meetly runs on macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later. It's a tiny native app (~8 MB), lives in your menu bar, and uses minimal resources.
